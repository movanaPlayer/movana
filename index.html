Movana for iOS

Offline Music, Organized Simply.

Movana is a local offline music player for iPhone that helps you import, organize, edit, and play music files stored on your own device.

Movana does not provide music downloads, and it does not upload, host, or share your music files on external servers.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

Core Features

Import Local Music

Use your music files stored on device for playback inside the app.

Create Playlists

Build and manage playlists for personal listening without cloud sync.

Edit Metadata

Update song title, artist, album, and other local music information.

Offline Playback

Enjoy your music library without requiring network streaming.
